Since it took some digging for me to find these, may you will find this useful

Triforce:

Dip 1: Not Used

Dip 2: Not Used

Dip 3: Not Used

Dip 4: On = Vertical, Off = Horizontal

Dip 5: On = C-Sync, Off = H,V Sync

Dip 6: On = 31KHz, Off = 15KHz
